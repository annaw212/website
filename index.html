I am a robotics software engineer with a foundation in back-end programming, and my driving passion lies in making the world more accessible through the use of robots.

Two additional realms I find fascinating and relevent are human-robot/computer interaction and UX design. In my capacity as a software engineer, my primary focus has been on back-end facets of the design process, meaning designing applications with an emphasis on logic and efficiency, and prioritizing technical considerations over user experience. However, I believe much of accessibility begins with good design, and I am excited to learn more about HRI and UX to further my understanding of how robotics can alter the world for the better.
